High-throughput multi-material DLP printing system

Mengxuan Gao; Likun Ye; Xue Lei; Yuhu Li; Yang Xu  |  2025, July 31   |  

Digital light processing (DLP) is a high-resolution, rapid 3D printing technology, but bottom-up multi-material approaches suffer from frequent resin switching, low print speed, and contamination. Here, we introduce a mobile liquid interface that enables multi-material structures to be printed block by block. This strategy preserves structural continuity, minimizes contamination, and boosts printing speed by 4.4–107× compared with conventional layer-by-layer switching, offering a scalable solution for complex multi-material DLP printing.

# Multi-material 3D printing # Photopolymerization 3D printing # Mobile interface printing # Multi-material slicing algorithm

Investigations on the oxidation behavior and removal mechanism of SiC/SiC composites by multi-pulse femtosecond laser ablation

Ning Zhou; Songmei Yuan; Mengxuan Gao; Wei Zhang; Jiaqi Zhang; Tianrui Hu  |  2023, Aug 19 Journal of Materials Research and Technology  |  IF: 6.6/Q1

This investigation analyzed the morphology, structure, composition, and plasma state of multi-pulse femtosecond laser ablated SiC/SiC composites from multiple perspectives using various static and in situ characterization methods. Further, the oxidation behavior and removal mechanism of SiC/SiC composites were studied qualitatively and quantitatively. Ultimately, the range of laser process parameters corresponding to multi-pulse femtosecond laser ablation of SiC/SiC composites without thermal removal as dominant was determined.

# Femtosecond laser machining of SiC/SiC composites # SiC/SiC ablation mechanisms

Laser ablation of SiC/SiC ceramic matrix composites: Morphological characterization, component evolution and oxidation mechanisms

Jiayong Wei; Ning Zhou; Mengxuan Gao; Tianrui Hu; Xiaoqi Li; Jiaqi Zhang; Wenzhao An; Wei Zhang; Songmei Yuan  |  2023, Dec 12 Corrosion Science  |  IF: 8.5/Q1

SiC/SiC composites play a pivotal role in aerospace and energy applications for their excellent resistance to high temperatures, corrosion, oxidation, and low density. This paper delves into the ablation behavior irradiated by a femtosecond laser, examining both qualitative and quantitative aspects through micro/nanostructure and component analysis. The findings reveal significant disparities in elements, compositions, and chemical states across the ablation zones, transition zones, and marginal zones. Moreover, a detailed discussion on the active and passive oxidation mechanisms in the ablation zone is presented. Notably, the oxidation product SiO2 acts as a barrier, impeding oxygen penetration into the material interior.

# Femtosecond laser machining # Oxidation mechanism # Ablation mechanism

Method and Device for Determining Laser Drilling Parameters of Composites

Songmei Yuan; Mengxuan Gao; Jiaqi Zhang; Jiayong Wei; Ning Zhou  |  2022, Jul 18 Invention Patent  |  CN115270616A

This invention provides a method and device for determining laser drilling parameters of composites: A training set is obtained from historical processing parameters and quality. An optimized neural network is trained on the training set to establish a processing parameter determination model. Multi-objective optimization is applied to processing parameters, and the optimized parameters are input into the model to obtain a Pareto optimal solution set. Based on the target processing results, an optimal parameter combination can be found.
